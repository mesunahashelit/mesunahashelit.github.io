The Beginning

Keren is a beautiful city in Eritrea, a country in Northeast Africa bordering the Red Sea, and it's the place where my story begins. I grew up in a diverse community speaking three different languages with a lot of childhood memories. Unfortunately, I was forced to leave my home land due to a barbaric regime.

1994

The Joyful Moment

After more than a decade of separation, I finally got reunited with my family in the United States. That was definitely one of the important life lessons I learned that it is such a blessing to have your loved ones around you & it should never be taken for granted!
